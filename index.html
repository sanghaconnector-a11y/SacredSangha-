what we're here for

We are humans with jobs, dishes and doings. Sacred Sangha provides a space where we can step out of doing and more into being — together.

“To settle into the awake awareness that we fundamentally are– living joyously and effortlessly from here.”

how a gathering moves

Each gathering is fluid and organic– utilizing ancient and modern practices in an easy and sincere way to address the needs of the day.

settle Meditation

We begin by arriving. A silent sit, guided or open — until we've actually landed. No rush, no performance.

inquire Self-Reflection

Journaling, quiet inquiry, or words shared aloud. A curious look at what's present.

embody Somatics

Yoga, qigong, gentle movement — awareness wearing a body for a while. Practice, not performance.

connect Conversation

Real talk, games, shared laughter — the part where spacious meets human. Connection as its own practice.

Come as you are. Nothing required but an open heart.

About

Sacred Sangha · Minneapolis

Created to make intentional, heart-centered connection easy and accessible.

No cost. No experience or special skills needed.
Just an open heart.

These gatherings are a co-creative experience — shaped by everyone who shows up. We sit, reflect, move, and talk together, trusting that something real happens when people gather with genuine intention.

Facilitated by
and fellow sangha members.

Bobby Lescarbeau

Facilitator · Sacred Sangha

Bobby began his mindfulness study in 2020 — a path that has taken him to the Rocky Mountains, the Pacific Northwest, and eventually to the Bahamas, where he met his wife. All the while, the journey kept leading further into the heart. Bobby is a certified yoga and meditation teacher, a graduate student in Mental Health Counseling, and a sincere practitioner of the mindful arts. It is his deep pleasure to walk alongside you on this path of opening into awareness.
